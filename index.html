What is TeamStation AI?

A system for building nearshore software teams in Latin America. It helps with search, vetting, hiring operations, security, and team governance.

What does a 10-year-old need to understand?

TeamStation helps a company find good software builders, check that they are really good, give them the right work setup, and help them join the team safely.

Why not just use a staffing vendor?

A staffing vendor often only sends resumes. TeamStation gives a fuller system, so you do not have to stitch together recruiters, payroll, laptops, security, and team management.
